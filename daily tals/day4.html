Born Details

Suryanarayana Raju, was a film producer, and his uncle, Krishnam Raju, is a veteran actor.Prabhas completed his schooling in Chennai and Bhimavaram, pursued intermediate education at Nalanda College, Hyderabad, and earned a Bachelor of Technology degree from Sri Chaitanya College, Hyderabad

The Baahubali-1 pivot and pan-India success
In 2015, Prabhas played a dual role as Shivudu/Mahendra Baahubali and Amarendra Baahubali in S.S. Rajamouli's epic action drama Baahubali: The Beginning (2015). With a budget of ₹180 crore ($28 million), it was the most expensive Indian film ever made at the time of release,[46] and became a record-breaking box office success. The film eventually grossed over ₹600 crore (US$63 million) worldwide, making it the highest-grossing Telugu film and the second highest-grossing Indian film at that point. It is one of the highest-grossing Indian film to date.[47][48][49] It received national and international acclaim, and started a new film movement called Pan-Indian films.
The Baahubali-2 pivot and pan-India success
In 2017, Prabhas reprised his role as Baahubali in the sequel Baahubali: The Conclusion (2017). The film continued the story of the epic saga and further solidified the pan-Indian film movement.

2020–present: Saalar and future projects

As of March 2026, Prabhas has several films in various stages of overlapping production. He is in the advanced stages of filming Fauzi (working title), a war-themed action drama directed by Hanu Raghavapudi. Set in 1940s British colonial India, the period drama features Prabhas as a rebellious soldier alongside an ensemble cast including Imanvi, Mithun Chakraborty, Jaya Prada, and Anupam Kher. Originally conceived as a single feature, the project has been expanded into a two-part saga. With the dialogue-centric portions largely wrapped, production is currently focused on completing major action sequences, targeting a theatrical release around the Dussehra festival in 2026.[108][109] Simultaneously, Prabhas is starring in the action-thriller Spirit, marking his first collaboration with director Sadeep Reddy Vanga. Principal photography began in late November 2025; following the completion of the first shooting schedule, Prabhas is slated to resume filming his remaining blocks in May 2026. The film features Prabhas as a fierce police officer combating an international crime syndicate, with Triptii Dimri, Vivek Oberoi, Prakash Raj, and Aishwarya Desai co-starring. The film will release on 5 March 2027

Awards and recognition

Prabhas has received several awards and nominations for his performances, including the Nandi Awards, Filmfare Awards South, and SIIMA Awards. He has also been recognized for his contribution to Indian cinema and has a significant fan following across the country.
